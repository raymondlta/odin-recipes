Steak

Description

This is going to be one of the best steak recipes you will ever see. We are aiming for a nice medium rare on the steak and will exclusively use ribeye steak for it wonderful marbling.

Do not be intimidated by this recipe. We will break this down step by step.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Start by dry brining the steak at least 30 minutes prior to cooking. This can also be done overnight. To start this process, simply place your raw steak onto a well venilated surface such as a drying rack and sprinkle the salt on both sides of the steak. Afterwards, place the steak into the fridge.
  2. About 30 minutes prior to cooking, bring the steak out of the fridge to allow it to reach room temperature. This will allow for even cooking.
  3. At around 25 minutes, start preheating the air fryer by setting it to 450 degrees Farenheit.
  4. Once the air fryer is up to temp, place the steak into the air fryer and set a time for 7 minutes.
  5. After 7 minutes, flip the steak over and start another timer for 3 minutes.
  6. After 3 minutes, place a knob of butter and the 2 cloves of garlic on top of the steak in the air fryer. Start another time for 3 minutes.
  7. After another 3 minutes, pull the steak out of the air fryer and allow to cool on a plate for 10 minutes.
  8. Once the 10 minutes is up, you may enjoy your nice medium to medium-rare steak.
