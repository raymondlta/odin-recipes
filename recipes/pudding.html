Pudding

Description

I have no idea how to make pudding. This is just a best guess on my end.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Egg Yolks
  • Corn Starch
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Cream egg yolks and sugar in a pot and place over medium heat. Make sure to keep stirring with a whisk to ensure smoothness.
  2. Once the mixture is pale and loose, incorporate milk slowly into the pot. Continue to whisk as milk is added into the pot.
  3. Allow the mixture to simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. After the 5 minutes, add the vanilla extract, salt, and corn starch. We would recommend mixing the corn starch into the vanilla extract first beforehand to ensure even distribution in the pot. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  5. Mixture should thicken and is ready once the marks left behind the whisk remain for more than 10 seconds.
  6. Pour into a heat safe bowl, place plastic wrap pressed directly into the mixture to prevent a skin from forming, and store in a fridge for a minimum of 4 hours.
  7. Once cooled, you may eat to your heart's content.
